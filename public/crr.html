About Sir CR Reddy Polytechnic College

Sir CR Reddy Polytechnic College was established with the vision of imparting quality technical education to students. The college is affiliated with the State Board of Technical Education and Training, Andhra Pradesh, and offers diploma programs in multiple branches of engineering and technology. The college is known for its academic excellence, experienced faculty, and modern infrastructure.

Academic Programs

The college offers a variety of diploma courses in engineering, designed to equip students with practical and theoretical knowledge to excel in their careers. Some of the key courses offered include:

  1. Diploma in Mechanical Engineering
  2. Diploma in Civil Engineering
  3. Diploma in Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  4. Diploma in Electronics and Communication Engineering
  5. Diploma in Computer Science Engineering
  6. These courses are carefully structured to provide students with a comprehensive understanding of the fundamentals, as well as hands-on experience in their chosen field of study.

Facilities and Infrastructure:

The college offers a range of modern facilities to support the learning and development of students:

  1. Classrooms: Spacious, well-lit, and equipped with modern teaching aids such as projectors and multimedia systems.
  3. Laboratories: The college has state-of-the-art laboratories for various engineering disciplines. These labs are designed to provide practical experience and enhance the theoretical knowledge gained in classrooms.
  4. Library: The college has a well-stocked library with a vast collection of textbooks, reference books, and journals. Students also have access to online databases and e-books.
  5. Computer Labs: The computer labs are equipped with high-speed internet access and modern computers to support students' learning and research activities.
  6. Sports and Recreation: The campus provides various sports facilities, including playgrounds for outdoor games and indoor facilities for recreation, promoting a healthy and balanced lifestyle for students.
  7. Placement and Career Opportunities: Sir CR Reddy Polytechnic College has a dedicated placement cell that plays an important role in preparing students for their careers. The placement cell works closely with leading industries and companies to organize campus recruitment drives and internships for students. The college has a strong track record of placing students in reputable companies, particularly in engineering and technical fields.
